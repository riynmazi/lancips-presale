Overview

Cipstrack detects and highlights newly emerging tokens with viral potential by analyzing social and on-chain signals in near real-time. It is designed to help traders, meme hunters, and researchers spot trends quickly — while reminding users that data insights are not investment advice.

Purpose: provide fast, data-driven visibility into tokens gaining traction across social channels and chains.

Core Concept / Mission

Cipstrack's mission is to surface early signals of token virality to the community. By aggregating engagement metrics and combining them with on-chain activity, Cipstrack produces a transparent "hype value" useful for monitoring trends.

  • Target audience: traders, meme hunters, data analysts, and researchers.
  • Value: faster discovery of high-engagement tokens and consolidated social + on-chain context.

Data Sources & Architecture

Cipstrack aggregates data from verified APIs and platforms to create a reliable multi-source signal pipeline.

  1. Fetch — collect public data from integrations such as Birdeye, Dexscreener, Twitter, and additional platforms as we expand.
  2. Analyze — normalize, deduplicate, and enrich raw metrics (mentions, likes, retweets, on-chain volume, liquidity, etc.).
  3. Score — compute viral and sentiment-based rankings using composite metrics.
  4. Output — serve results to the dashboard, charts, and ranking endpoints (and future public API).

Outputs are presented on a user-friendly dashboard with charts and ranking tables to make trend signals actionable.

Viral Score Calculation

The Viral Score is a composite metric derived from social engagement signals and on-chain activity. It is intended to indicate momentum and hype, not fundamental value.

Factors include:

  • Trading volume (on-chain)
  • Mentions and name-drops (social platforms)
  • Likes, retweets, and comments
  • Engagement velocity (how fast mentions & interactions grow)
  • Cross-chain reach and liquidity signals

We avoid publishing the exact proprietary formula, but the scoring engine uses multi-source normalization to reduce single-source manipulation.

Update Frequency & Performance

Cipstrack refreshes aggregated data every 1 minute, providing near real-time visibility into fast-moving trends. To maintain performance we use caching and queue-based workers to prevent overload on both internal systems and third-party APIs.

User Interaction

Users can interact with Cipstrack via the dashboard and soon via public API endpoints. Key interactions include:

  • Search for tokens or chains
  • Filter by score, volume, chain, or category
  • Viral news feed highlighting tokens with rapid engagement growth
  • Rankings and token detail views with combined social + on-chain context

Security, Transparency & The $LCIP Token

Cipstrack is a core feature of the Lancips / $LCIP ecosystem and is powered by the official $LCIP token. While $LCIP supports platform utility, Cipstrack itself operates as an analytical service — not an investment product.

Security Practices

  1. Verified data sources: Only trusted APIs (Birdeye, Dexscreener, Twitter, etc.) are used to reduce risk from anonymous or manipulated feeds.
  2. Read-only data flow: Cipstrack performs read-only aggregation of public data and does not execute transactions or access user wallets.
  3. API gateway protection: Rate limiting, authentication for internal endpoints, and monitoring to prevent abuse and DDoS.
  4. Data integrity & anti-manipulation: Cross-source validation and signal weighting reduce impact of isolated fake engagement.
  5. Transparency: Source attribution and update timestamps are shown where possible; $LCIP is not used to alter scoring outcomes.

In summary: Cipstrack focuses on secure, verifiable signals and protects platform availability while making scoring decisions transparent and auditable for users.
